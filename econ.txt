Supply and Demand - The relationship between the availability of a good (supply) and the desire for it (demand), which determines its price.
Opportunity Cost - The value of the next best alternative that is forgone when making a choice.
Inflation - The rate at which the general level of prices for goods and services rises, reducing purchasing power.
Gross Domestic Product (GDP) - The total value of all goods and services produced within a country in a given period.
Market Equilibrium - The point at which supply and demand are balanced, resulting in stable prices.
